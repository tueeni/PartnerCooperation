About Our Process

The process begins with company partners filling out a registration form. Once the form is completed, it is submitted for processing by a server. The server then generates a PDF version of the form. This PDF is subsequently emailed to Kaspi. Upon receipt, Kaspi reviews the form and provides a response. The server, upon receiving Kaspi's response, processes it to determine whether the application has been accepted or rejected. This entire sequence ensures that partner registrations are efficiently managed and responded to in a timely manner.
